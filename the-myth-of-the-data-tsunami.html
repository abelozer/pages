The Information Filter

A common misconception is that connected cars stream everything to the cloud. In reality, cars are Edge Datacenters. They filter out 99.9% of routine driving data locally, uploading only critical anomalies and metadata.

Putting Bandwidth in Context

Network operators fear a "Data Tsunami" from vehicles. However, when we compare the actual telemetry load against typical consumer behavior, the vehicle appears negligible. The real bandwidth hog is high-definition video streaming.
