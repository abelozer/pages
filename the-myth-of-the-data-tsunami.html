The 4 Terabyte Funnel

Modern Software Defined Vehicles (SDVs) are often described as "Data Centers on Wheels." While it is true that sensors generate massive amounts of raw information, the "Edge Computing" architecture ensures that 99.9% of this data never leaves the chassis. The car processes vision and decision-making locally, transmitting only metadata and specific "corner cases" to the cloud.
